# 📖 LuxeDrive: User & Admin Manual
**Version:** 1.0.0

---

## 👤 Part 1: For the Customer (User)
### 1. Account Setup
*   **Registration:** Visit `midhunshaj.in/register` or use the **Google One-Tap Login** for instant access.
*   **Profile:** Once logged in, you can update your profile and see your "Status" (Verified/Unverified).

### 2. Booking a Vehicle
*   **Browse:** Go to the "Fleet" page to see available cars.
*   **Selection:** Click on a car to see the luxury specifications.
*   **Checkout:** Select your dates, calculate the price, and click "Book Now."
*   **Payment:** Pay securely via the **Razorpay** popup.

### 3. Tracking
*   Go to "My Bookings" to see if your request is *Pending*, *Accepted*, or *Completed*.

---

## 🛠️ Part 2: For the Dealer (Admin)
### 1. Accessing the Dashboard
*   Log in with an Admin account and navigate to `/admin`.

### 2. Managing Fleet
*   **Add Vehicle:** Upload high-quality photos, set the daily price, and fuel type.
*   **Inventory:** Toggle the "Availability" switch. If a car is under maintenance, turn it off so users can't book it.

### 3. Approving Bookings
*   **Request List:** View all incoming booking requests.
*   **Approval:** Once you click "Accept," the system will **automatically email the customer** confirming their rental.
*   **Completion:** Once the car is returned, mark the booking as "Completed" to free up the vehicle in the inventory.

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting
*   **Payment Failure:** Ensure you have enough balance and a stable internet connection. Razorpay will auto-refund failed sessions.
*   **Email Not Received:** Check your "Spam" folder. The system uses a secure SMTP protocol.
